The Essex Consort: A Celebration of St. Crispin’s Day
September 14, 2013 6PM

The Essex Consort is one of the leading ensembles specializing in the performance
of Medieval and Renaissance music. Drawing on a repertoire of several centuries,
the group has long been an audience favorite in its home city of New York, and
annual tours include sold-out performances at major concert halls coast to coast.
In the spirit and pageantry of medieval church dramas and mystery plays,
eight singers and five instrumentalists playing reproductions of medieval
instruments recount the events of St Crispin's Day—a program combining
solemn and festive music, processions and simple gestures to dramatize
the narrative as conveyed by music manuscripts and illuminated
miniatures of the Middle Ages.

Tickets: Adults, $15; seniors, $10; students, $5 (with ID)

Midland City
 October 25, 2013 6PM

 Midland City, with lead guitarist Philip Castle, breathes new life into the all-string
instrumentation of violin, bass and guitars. Hearing the ensemble live carries the
listener back to the 1930s, to the small, smoky jazz clubs of Paris or the refined
of the famous Hotel Ritz. For this performance, Midland City presents Seed Crystal,
an evening of Acid Jazz and instrumentation inspired by the Paris café scene.
A wine tasting featuring spirits from local wineries and microbrews will follow
the event, Proof of age required.

Tickets: Adults, $25; seniors, $10; students, $10 (with ID)
En Pointe
November 24, 2013 7:30PM

Celebrating its 25th anniversary this year, En Pointe American Dance Theater has
earned a reputation as one of the most acclaimed troupes for modern dance.
En Pointe, is universally renowned for merging the spirit and energy of the country’s
best young dance talent with the passion and creative vision of today’s most outstanding
emerging choreographers. En Pointe’s Artistic Director, George Bergeron, appointed by the
troupes founder in 1994, has established this ensemble as one of the most popular
dance companies in the country.

Adults, $20; seniors, $15; students, $5 (with ID)

December 30, 2013 6:00PM

Hazel Crosby
February 17, 2014 7:00PM

Award-winning artist, Hazel Crosby established her career as a compelling
singer/songwriter with the debut releases Busy, Busy, Busy and Ice10,

Arthur Barnhouse: “Forces of the Mind”
March 1, 2014 8:30PM

Arthur Barnhouse, Grammy Award-winning stand-up comedian and satirist,
is one of the most prolific and popular performers working today. His live
performances provide a cathartic release of anger and disillusionment
for his audience. He is a passionate performer who is more angry-optimist
than mean-spirited curmudgeon. Barnhouse is the rare comic who can
cause an audience to laugh themselves into incontinence while making
compelling points about the absurdity of our world.

Content may not be suitable for younger audiences!!
